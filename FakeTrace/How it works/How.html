How does FakeTrace work?

To use the FakeTrace, follow these simple steps:

1

Upload the Image:

  • Navigate to the FakeTrace on our website.
  • Click the 'Upload Image' button and select the image you want to analyze from your device.
2

Analyze the Image:

  • Once the image is uploaded, the FakeTrace will begin analyzing it. This process involves examining various attributes and patterns within the image.
3

Receive the Results:

  • After the analysis is complete, the FakeTrace will provide you with the results, indicating whether the image is likely to be Real or DeepFake.
